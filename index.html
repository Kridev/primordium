Primordium

an emergent particle-life ecosystem

Every dot belongs to a coloured species. An attraction matrix decides how strongly each colour is pulled toward or pushed from every other — and below a short range everything repels, so nothing collapses. That's the entire model. The cells, membranes, chasers and oscillators you see were never coded; they're just what those rules do.

Try this: hit 🎲 Surprise me for a fresh world, click cells in the matrix to rewrite the laws of physics, drag to stir, double-click to drop a colony, or turn on 📺 Auto and just watch.
